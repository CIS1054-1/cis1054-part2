About our Restaurant

Los Pollos Hermanos is a chain of restaurants spread over 30 countries and based in the main cities around the world such as NY, Paris, Madrid, Rome, etc. We have a wide variety of products of the best possible selection and also with a homemade touch. Don't wait and come and eat at Los Pollos Hermanos.

Our Staff

Our managing director, Gustavo Fring, is an Italian-Hispanic entrepreneur who opened his first restaurant more than 15 years ago with his own money. From the very beginning, he has always been concerned about customer service, which he considers essential and has instilled these values in his employees, who will welcome you at the entrance with open arms.
